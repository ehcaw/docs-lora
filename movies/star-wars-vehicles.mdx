### X-wing starfighter

The T-65B X-wing is the Rebel Alliance's signature multi-purpose starfighter. Luke Skywalker famously flew an X-wing during the Battle of Yavin to destroy the Death Star.

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Manufacturer** | Incom Corporation |
| **Length** | 12.5 meters |
| **Armament** | 4 laser cannons, proton torpedoes |
| **Crew** | 1 pilot + astromech droid |

### TIE Fighter

The Twin Ion Engine (TIE) fighter is the standard Imperial starfighter, recognizable by its distinctive scream and hexagonal solar panels.

<Info>
TIE fighters lack shields and hyperdrive, making them expendable and reliant on numerical superiority.
</Info>

## Capital ships

### Rebel Alliance

- **Mon Calamari Cruiser** - Primary Rebel capital ship, like the *Home One*
- **Corellian Corvette** - Blockade runners like the *Tantive IV*
- **Nebulon-B Frigate** - Medical and escort vessel

### Galactic Empire

- **Star Destroyer** - The Empire's iconic wedge-shaped warship
- **Super Star Destroyer** - Massive command ships like the *Executor*
- **Death Star** - Planet-destroying battle station

| Ship class | Length |

| **Super Star Destroyer** | 19,000 meters |

### Millennium Falcon

The fastest hunk of junk in the galaxy. This modified YT-1300 Corellian freighter is piloted by Han Solo and Chewbacca.

> "She may not look like much, but she's got it where it counts." — Han Solo

Notable features:
- Modified hyperdrive capable of 0.5 past lightspeed
- Quad laser cannons
- Smuggling compartments
- Famous for completing the Kessel Run in less than 12 parsecs

## Ground vehicles

### Imperial

- **AT-AT** - All Terrain Armored Transport, used in the Battle of Hoth
- **AT-ST** - All Terrain Scout Transport, "chicken walker"
- **Imperial Speeder Bike** - Fast reconnaissance vehicle

### Rebel and civilian

- **T-47 Airspeeder** - Snowspeeders used to tow cable AT-ATs
- **Landspeeder** - Luke's X-34 desert vehicle
- **Podracer** - High-speed racing vehicles from Tatooine

## Clone Wars era

- **Jedi Starfighter** - Delta-7 and Eta-2 interceptors
- **Republic Gunship** - LAAT/i infantry transport
- **Venator-class Star Destroyer** - Republic capital ship
- **Droid Starfighter** - Separatist Vulture droids

<Info>
Many Clone Wars era designs influenced the ships seen in the original trilogy, showing the evolution of technology in the galaxy.
</Info>

## Cultural impact

Star Wars vehicles have become design icons, inspiring real aerospace concepts and countless toys and models. The distinctive sounds of TIE fighters and the Millennium Falcon's hyperdrive are instantly recognizable worldwide.
