# Star Wars vehicles

The Star Wars universe features some of the most memorable vehicles in science fiction. From nimble starfighters to massive capital ships, these machines play crucial roles throughout the saga.

## Capital ships

The galaxy's largest vessels serve as flagships, carriers, and symbols of military power.

### Galactic Empire

- **Star Destroyer** - The wedge-shaped backbone of the Imperial Navy, 1,600 meters long
- **Super Star Destroyer** - Massive command ships like Darth Vader's *Executor* (19,000 meters)
- **Death Star** - Moon-sized battle stations capable of destroying planets

### Rebel Alliance / New Republic

- **Mon Calamari cruiser** - Converted luxury liners turned warships, like *Home One*
- **Nebulon-B frigate** - Medical and escort vessels
- **CR90 corvette** - Fast blockade runners like the *Tantive IV*

## Iconic vessels

<Info>
The Millennium Falcon, piloted by Han Solo and Chewbacca, made the Kessel Run in less than twelve parsecs.
</Info>

### Millennium Falcon

The most famous ship in Star Wars, this modified YT-1300 freighter features:

- Customized hyperdrive (Class 0.5)
- Concealed smuggling compartments
- Quad laser cannons
- Survived both Death Star battles

### Slave I

Boba Fett's distinctive patrol craft, known for its rotating cockpit and arsenal of weapons.

## Ground vehicles

Star Wars also features memorable ground-based vehicles:

- **AT-AT** - All Terrain Armored Transport, the Empire's walking assault vehicle
- **AT-ST** - Smaller scout walker ("chicken walker")
- **Speeder bikes** - Fast reconnaissance vehicles used on Endor
- **Sandcrawler** - Jawa transport on Tatooine
- **Landspeeder** - Luke's X-34 civilian hovercraft

## Legacy

Star Wars vehicles have become cultural icons, inspiring countless toys, video games, and real-world aerospace designs. The distinctive silhouettes of the X-wing and Star Destroyer remain instantly recognizable decades after their debut.
