### X-wing starfighter

The T-65B X-wing became the symbol of the Rebel Alliance. Luke Skywalker flew an X-wing when he destroyed the first Death Star.

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Manufacturer** | Incom Corporation |
| **Length** | 12.5 meters |
| **Armament** | 4 laser cannons, proton torpedoes |
| **Crew** | 1 pilot, 1 astromech droid |

### TIE Fighter

The Twin Ion Engine fighter serves as the backbone of the Imperial Navy. Fast but lacking shields and hyperdrive.

<Info>
TIE stands for "Twin Ion Engine," referring to the distinctive engine design that gives these fighters their iconic sound.
</Info>

## Capital ships

### Star Destroyer

The Imperial Star Destroyer represents the might of the Empire. At 1,600 meters long, these ships carry thousands of troops and dozens of TIE fighters.

### Mon Calamari cruisers

The Rebel Alliance's largest warships, converted from civilian vessels by the Mon Calamari people.

| Ship | Role |
|------|------|
| **Home One** | Admiral Ackbar's flagship |
| **Profundity** | Raddus's ship at Scarif |
| **Raddus** | Resistance flagship |

## Iconic vehicles

### Millennium Falcon

Han Solo's legendary Corellian freighter, capable of making the Kessel Run in less than twelve parsecs.

> "She may not look like much, but she's got it where it counts, kid." — Han Solo

### Slave I

Boba Fett's distinctive Firespray-class patrol craft, known for its rotating cockpit and powerful weapons.

### AT-AT walker

The All Terrain Armored Transport is a four-legged combat walker used by the Empire. Featured prominently in the Battle of Hoth.

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Height** | 22.5 meters |
| **Crew** | 5 (pilot, gunner, commander, 2 engineers) |
| **Troops** | 40 soldiers |

### Landspeeder

Luke Skywalker's X-34 landspeeder used hover technology to glide across Tatooine's desert terrain.

## Prequel-era vehicles

- **Jedi starfighter** - Compact interceptors used by Jedi Knights
- **N-1 starfighter** - Naboo's sleek yellow fighters
- **Republic gunship** - LAAT/i used in the Clone Wars
- **Droid fighters** - Separatist automated starfighters
