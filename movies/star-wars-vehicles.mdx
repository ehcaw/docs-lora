### X-wing starfighter

The T-65B X-wing became the symbol of the Rebel Alliance. Luke Skywalker flew an X-wing during the Battle of Yavin to destroy the first Death Star.

| Detail | Specification |
|--------|---------------|
| **Manufacturer** | Incom Corporation |
| **Length** | 12.5 meters |
| **Armament** | 4 laser cannons, proton torpedoes |
| **Crew** | 1 pilot, 1 astromech droid |

## Imperial starfighters

### TIE fighter

The Twin Ion Engine fighter serves as the Empire's primary combat spacecraft. Fast and maneuverable but lacking shields or hyperdrive.

### TIE variants

- **TIE Interceptor** - Enhanced speed and firepower
- **TIE Bomber** - Heavy ordnance delivery
- **TIE Advanced** - Darth Vader's personal fighter with shields
- **TIE Defender** - Elite fighter with shields and hyperdrive

<Info>
TIE fighters produce their distinctive scream from their twin ion engines, one of the most recognizable sounds in cinema.
</Info>

### Imperial Star Destroyer

The wedge-shaped Star Destroyer became synonymous with Imperial might. At 1,600 meters long, these vessels carried thousands of troops and dozens of TIE fighters.

### Super Star Destroyer

The Executor-class Super Star Destroyer measured 19 kilometers long. Darth Vader's flagship *Executor* was destroyed during the Battle of Endor.

## Freighters and transports

### Millennium Falcon

Han Solo's modified YT-1300 light freighter is perhaps the most famous ship in the Star Wars universe.

> "She may not look like much, but she's got it where it counts, kid." — Han Solo

| Detail | Specification |
|--------|---------------|
| **Class** | YT-1300 light freighter |
| **Length** | 34.75 meters |
| **Crew** | 2 (minimum) |
| **Speed** | Fastest ship in the galaxy |

## Ground vehicles

### AT-AT walker

The All Terrain Armored Transport towered over battlefields at 22.5 meters tall. Featured prominently in the Battle of Hoth.

### Other Imperial walkers

- **AT-ST** - All Terrain Scout Transport ("chicken walker")
- **AT-M6** - Upgraded walker in the First Order era
- **AT-ACT** - Cargo transport variant

### Speeder bikes

Fast reconnaissance vehicles used by both Imperial scouts and Rebel forces. The forest chase on Endor showcased their speed and maneuverability.

## Podracers

<Info>
Podracing appears prominently in *The Phantom Menace*, where young Anakin Skywalker wins his freedom.
</Info>

These high-speed racing vehicles reached speeds over 900 kilometers per hour, requiring Force sensitivity or exceptional reflexes to pilot.

## Legacy

Star Wars vehicles have inspired countless designs in other media and become cultural icons. From the Millennium Falcon's distinctive silhouette to the menacing profile of a Star Destroyer, these designs remain instantly recognizable decades after their creation.
