# Tom and Jerry

**Tom and Jerry** is an American animated media franchise and series of comedy short films created in 1940 by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera. It centers on the rivalry between the titular characters: a cat named Tom and a mouse named Jerry.

### Supporting characters

- **Spike** - A gray bulldog who is often annoyed by Tom
- **Tyke** - Spike's son, a small gray puppy
- **Nibbles/Tuffy** - A small gray mouse and Jerry's nephew
- **Mammy Two Shoes** - Tom's owner (early cartoons)
- **Butch** - A scruffy black alley cat and Tom's rival

## Premise

The series depicts Tom's endless pursuit of Jerry, whose cleverness allows him to escape and often turn the tables on his feline adversary. Despite their rivalry, the two occasionally team up against common enemies or help each other in times of need.

## Production history

### Golden Age (1940-1958)

The original theatrical shorts were produced by MGM's cartoon studio. Hanna and Barbera directed 114 shorts during this period, winning seven Academy Awards for Animated Short Film:

1. *The Yankee Doodle Mouse* (1943)
2. *Mouse Trouble* (1944)
3. *Quiet Please!* (1945)
4. *The Cat Concerto* (1947)
5. *The Little Orphan* (1948)
6. *The Two Mouseketeers* (1952)
7. *Johann Mouse* (1953)

### Later productions

Following the original series, Tom and Jerry appeared in numerous productions:

- **Gene Deitch era** (1961-1962) - 13 shorts
- **Chuck Jones era** (1963-1967) - 34 shorts
- **Filmation series** (1975)
- **The Tom and Jerry Show** (1975)
- **Tom and Jerry Kids** (1990-1993)
- **Tom and Jerry Tales** (2006-2008)

## Cultural impact

Tom and Jerry became one of the most beloved animated series in history, influencing generations of animators and entertaining audiences worldwide.

<Info>
Tom and Jerry holds the record for the most Academy Awards won by a theatrical animated series.
</Info>

## Legacy

The franchise has expanded to include:

- Over 13 theatrical and direct-to-video films
- Multiple television series and spin-offs
- Video games
- Merchandise and theme park attractions
- A live-action/animated hybrid film (2021)

## Memorable moments

> The classic "chase" formula combined with creative visual gags and musical synchronization made Tom and Jerry a timeless masterpiece of animation.

The series is known for its slapstick comedy, minimal dialogue, and expressive animation that transcends language barriers, making it popular in over 80 countries.
