# Tom and Jerry

**Tom and Jerry** (2021) is a live-action/animated comedy film produced by Warner Animation Group. Directed by Tim Story, it brings the classic cartoon rivals into the real world for a new generation of fans.

## Plot summary

When Jerry moves into New York City's finest hotel on the eve of the wedding of the century, the desperate event planner Kayla hires Tom to get rid of him. Their cat-and-mouse battle threatens to destroy her career, the wedding, and possibly the hotel itself.

### Main characters

- **Tom** - A determined house cat who takes a job at the Royal Gate Hotel
- **Jerry** - A clever mouse who has made the hotel his new home
- **Kayla** - An ambitious event planner who hires Tom
- **Terence** - The hotel's deputy general manager
- **Ben** - Kayla's coworker and friend

## Production

### Development

The film went through various iterations over the years, with different studios and directors attached. Warner Bros. finally moved forward with the project in 2018, with Tim Story directing.

### Animation style

The filmmakers chose to maintain the classic 2D animated look of Tom and Jerry rather than making them photorealistic. This decision preserved the characters' iconic appearances while placing them in live-action environments.

### Filming

Principal photography took place in London in 2019. The hotel scenes were filmed at various locations, including Savoy Hotel.

## Reception

### Critical response

The film received mixed reviews from critics. While praised for its nostalgic appeal and the chemistry between the animated characters, some felt the human storyline was less engaging.

### Box office performance

Despite releasing during the COVID-19 pandemic simultaneously in theaters and on HBO Max, the film performed reasonably well:

- Opening weekend: $13.7 million domestic
- Worldwide gross: $136.3 million
- One of the top-performing films during the pandemic era

## Legacy

Tom and Jerry introduced the classic characters to a new generation while honoring their slapstick comedy roots.

## Classic rivalry

> The film continues the timeless tradition of Tom and Jerry's chaotic chases and elaborate schemes, proving some rivalries never get old.

The movie features numerous callbacks to classic Tom and Jerry cartoons, including:

- Iconic chase sequences
- Elaborate traps and gadgets
- Physical comedy without dialogue from the main duo
- Cameos from other classic Hanna-Barbera characters
