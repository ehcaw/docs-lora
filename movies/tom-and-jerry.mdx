# Tom and Jerry

**Tom and Jerry** is an American animated media franchise and series of comedy short films created by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera for Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer. The series centers on the rivalry between its two main characters, Tom the cat and Jerry the mouse.

## Overview

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Created by** | William Hanna and Joseph Barbera |
| **First short** | February 10, 1940 |
| **Studio** | MGM Cartoon Studio (original) |
| **Total shorts** | 164 theatrical shorts (1940-1967) |
| **Academy Awards** | 7 wins |

### Supporting characters

- **Spike** - A gray bulldog who often protects Jerry from Tom
- **Tyke** - Spike's son, a small puppy
- **Butch** - A black alley cat and Tom's rival
- **Nibbles/Tuffy** - A small gray mouse and Jerry's nephew
- **Mammy Two Shoes** - Tom's owner (early shorts)

## Theatrical eras

The original theatrical shorts span several distinct production periods:

1. *Hanna-Barbera era* (1940-1958) - The classic MGM shorts
2. *Gene Deitch era* (1961-1962) - Produced in Prague
3. *Chuck Jones era* (1963-1967) - Final theatrical shorts

## Signature style

The series is famous for its slapstick comedy, minimal dialogue, and creative cartoon violence. The chase sequences feature elaborate setups and comedic timing that influenced generations of animators.

<Info>
Tom and Jerry won seven Academy Awards for Best Animated Short Film, more than any other animated series.
</Info>

## Cultural impact

Tom and Jerry became one of the most successful and enduring animated franchises in history:

- Television series and specials
- Feature films (theatrical and direct-to-video)
- Crossover films with other properties
- Video games and merchandise
- Theme park attractions worldwide

## Legacy

> "Is there anything funnier than a cat chasing a mouse?"

For over 80 years, Tom and Jerry has entertained audiences with its timeless comedy and universal appeal. The characters remain cultural icons, proving that the simple premise of cat versus mouse can produce endless entertainment.
