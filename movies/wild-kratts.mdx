# Wild Kratts

**Wild Kratts** is an American-Canadian educational animated series created by Chris and Martin Kratt. The series combines animation with live-action segments to teach children about wildlife biology and zoology.

## Overview

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Created by** | Chris Kratt and Martin Kratt |
| **First episode** | January 3, 2011 |
| **Network** | PBS Kids |
| **Seasons** | 6+ seasons |
| **Episodes** | 150+ episodes |

## Main characters

- **Chris Kratt** - The younger Kratt brother who wears green and specializes in animal behavior
- **Martin Kratt** - The older Kratt brother who wears blue and focuses on animal naming
- **Aviva Corcovado** - The team's inventor who creates the Creature Power Suits
- **Koki** - The computer expert and communications specialist
- **Jimmy Z** - The pilot of the Tortuga HQ

## The Creature Power Suits

The signature element of the show is the Creature Power Suits, which allow the Kratt brothers to transform into animals and experience life from their perspective.

<Info>
Each Creature Power Suit requires a Power Disc programmed by Aviva and a touch from the actual animal to activate.
</Info>

## Notable episodes

1. *Mom of a Croc* - Learning about crocodile maternal care
2. *Flight of the Draco* - Exploring flying lizards
3. *Creature Power Challenge* - Competition between the brothers
4. *A Huge Orange Problem* - Orangutan conservation

## Villains

The Wild Kratts team faces several recurring antagonists who threaten wildlife:

- **Zach Varmitech** - A tech entrepreneur who wants to use animals as robots
- **Donita Donata** - A fashion designer who captures animals for clothing
- **Gourmand** - A chef who hunts endangered animals for exotic dishes
- **Paisley Paver** - A developer who destroys habitats for construction

<Note>
The villains represent real-world threats to wildlife, teaching children about conservation challenges.
</Note>

## The Tortuga HQ

The Wild Kratts team operates from the Tortuga, a turtle-shaped aircraft that serves as their mobile headquarters. It contains:

- A lab for Aviva's inventions
- Computer systems for research
- Storage for Creature Power Discs
- Living quarters for the team

## Legacy

> "Activate Creature Powers!"

Wild Kratts has become one of the most popular educational shows for children, inspiring interest in wildlife and conservation. The Kratt brothers previously created *Kratts' Creatures* and *Zoboomafoo*, building a legacy of wildlife education spanning decades.
