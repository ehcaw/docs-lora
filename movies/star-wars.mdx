# Star Wars

**Star Wars** is an American epic space opera multimedia franchise created by George Lucas. The franchise began with the eponymous 1977 film and quickly became a worldwide pop culture phenomenon.

## Overview

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Created by** | George Lucas |
| **First film** | May 25, 1977 |
| **Studio** | Lucasfilm |
| **Distributor** | 20th Century Fox (1977-2019), Walt Disney Studios (2019-present) |
| **Total films** | 12 theatrical releases |

## Main characters

- **Luke Skywalker** - A farm boy who becomes a legendary Jedi Knight
- **Darth Vader** - The iconic Sith Lord and fallen Jedi
- **Princess Leia Organa** - A leader of the Rebel Alliance
- **Han Solo** - A smuggler who becomes a hero of the Rebellion
- **Obi-Wan Kenobi** - A wise Jedi Master
- **Yoda** - The ancient and powerful Jedi Grand Master
- **Emperor Palpatine** - The Dark Lord of the Sith

## The Force

The Force is an energy field created by all living things that binds the galaxy together. Force-sensitive individuals can tap into its power, with practitioners divided into two main orders:

## Spin-off films

Beyond the main saga, the franchise includes:

- **Rogue One: A Star Wars Story** (2016) - The mission to steal the Death Star plans
- **Solo: A Star Wars Story** (2018) - The origin story of Han Solo

## Cultural impact

<Info>
Star Wars revolutionized the film industry with groundbreaking visual effects, merchandise licensing, and expanded universe storytelling.
</Info>

The franchise has influenced countless filmmakers and has become embedded in global popular culture. Iconic quotes like "May the Force be with you" and "I am your father" have transcended the films.

## Expanded universe

The Star Wars universe extends beyond films to include:

- **Television series** - The Mandalorian, The Clone Wars, Rebels, Ahsoka, Andor
- **Books and comics** - Hundreds of novels and graphic novels
- **Video games** - Knights of the Old Republic, Jedi: Fallen Order, Battlefront
- **Theme park attractions** - Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge at Disney parks

## Legacy

> In a galaxy far, far away, George Lucas created not just a film, but a mythology for the modern age.

Star Wars continues to captivate audiences across generations, with new content regularly expanding the beloved universe.
