The Star Wars galaxy contains thousands of unique worlds, each with its own environment, culture, and significance to the saga.

### Coruscant

The political center of the galaxy, Coruscant serves as the capital of both the Galactic Republic and later the Empire. Its entire surface is covered by a sprawling cityscape.

### Alderaan

A peaceful, mountainous world known for its beauty and culture. Alderaan was destroyed by the Death Star in *A New Hope*, becoming a symbol of Imperial tyranny.

## Desert worlds

### Tatooine

Luke Skywalker's homeworld, a harsh desert planet orbiting twin suns. Key location in both trilogies.

<Info>
Tatooine scenes were filmed in Tunisia, and the planet's name comes from the Tunisian city of Tataouine.
</Info>

- Home of the Skywalker family
- Jabba the Hutt's palace
- Mos Eisley spaceport ("a wretched hive of scum and villainy")

### Jakku

A scavenger world where Rey discovers her connection to the Force. Site of a major battle between the Empire and New Republic.

## Ice and snow worlds

### Hoth

The frozen planet where the Rebel Alliance established Echo Base. Location of the iconic AT-AT walker battle in *The Empire Strikes Back*.

### Dagobah

A remote swamp world where Yoda trained Luke Skywalker. Strong with the Force, it concealed Yoda's presence from the Empire.

> "Strong with the Force, this place is." — Yoda

## Volcanic and lava worlds

### Mustafar

A volcanic planet where Anakin Skywalker's transformation into Darth Vader was completed. Site of the legendary duel between Anakin and Obi-Wan.

## Ocean worlds

### Kamino

A stormy ocean planet and home to the cloning facilities that produced the Republic's clone army.

### Ahch-To

The hidden world where Luke Skywalker lived in exile and where Rey sought his guidance. Location of the first Jedi Temple.

## Planetary systems

The galaxy is divided into regions:

- **Core Worlds** — Wealthy, politically powerful (Coruscant, Alderaan)
- **Mid Rim** — Moderate development (Naboo, Kashyyyk)
- **Outer Rim** — Frontier worlds, less law and order (Tatooine, Hoth)
- **Unknown Regions** — Uncharted space (Exegol, Ahch-To)
