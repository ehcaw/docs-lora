### Coruscant

The entire planet is covered by a single city spanning thousands of levels. It serves as the capital of both the Galactic Republic and the Empire.

- **Climate**: Temperate (artificial)
- **Notable locations**: Jedi Temple, Senate Building, lower levels
- **Significance**: Political center of the galaxy

### Alderaan

A beautiful world known for its art, culture, and pacifist government. Its destruction by the Death Star became a rallying point for the Rebel Alliance.

### Tatooine

<Info>
Tatooine has appeared in more Star Wars films than any other planet.
</Info>

A harsh desert world on the Outer Rim, Tatooine is home to moisture farmers, Jawas, and Tusken Raiders. Key locations include:

- Mos Eisley cantina
- Jabba's Palace
- Lars homestead

## Ice and snow worlds

### Hoth

The Rebel Alliance's Echo Base was located on this frozen planet. The Battle of Hoth in *The Empire Strikes Back* forced the Rebels to evacuate.

- **Climate**: Frozen
- **Wildlife**: Wampas, tauntauns
- **Notable event**: Battle of Hoth

### Dagobah

A remote swamp world where Yoda trained Luke Skywalker. Its strong connection to the Force made it an ideal hiding place for the Jedi Master.

> "Strong with the dark side of the Force, a place it is." — Yoda

## Volcanic and lava worlds

### Mustafar

A volcanic planet where Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi fought their fateful duel. The planet later became the site of Darth Vader's fortress.

## Ocean planets

### Kamino

An ocean world with constant storms, home to the cloning facilities that created the Republic's clone army.

- **Notable feature**: Tipoca City (stilt city above the ocean)
- **Inhabitants**: Kaminoans (skilled cloners)

## Planets by trilogy

### Original trilogy locations

1. **Tatooine** - Luke's home, Mos Eisley
2. **Yavin 4** - Rebel base, Death Star attack launch point
3. **Hoth** - Echo Base, Battle of Hoth
4. **Dagobah** - Yoda's exile, Luke's training
5. **Bespin** - Cloud City, Han frozen in carbonite
6. **Endor** - Shield generator, final battle

### Prequel trilogy locations

1. **Naboo** - Padmé's homeworld, Trade Federation invasion
2. **Coruscant** - Jedi Temple, Senate
3. **Kamino** - Clone army creation
4. **Geonosis** - First battle of the Clone Wars
5. **Mustafar** - Anakin vs Obi-Wan duel

### Sequel trilogy locations

1. **Jakku** - Rey's home, Millennium Falcon found
2. **Takodana** - Maz Kanata's castle
3. **Starkiller Base** - First Order superweapon
4. **Ahch-To** - First Jedi Temple, Luke's exile
5. **Exegol** - Sith homeworld, final battle
