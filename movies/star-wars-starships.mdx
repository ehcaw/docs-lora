### X-wing starfighter

The workhorse of the Rebel Alliance, the T-65B X-wing is a versatile snubfighter known for its distinctive S-foils that open into an "X" formation during combat.

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Manufacturer** | Incom Corporation |
| **Length** | 12.5 meters |
| **Armament** | 4 laser cannons, proton torpedoes |
| **Notable pilots** | Luke Skywalker, Wedge Antilles, Poe Dameron |

### Millennium Falcon

The fastest hunk of junk in the galaxy, this modified YT-1300 light freighter made the Kessel Run in less than twelve parsecs.

<Info>
The Millennium Falcon's distinctive asymmetrical design was inspired by a hamburger with an olive on the side.
</Info>

### Other Rebel ships

- **Y-wing** - Durable bomber used in trench runs
- **A-wing** - Fast interceptor
- **B-wing** - Heavy assault starfighter
- **U-wing** - Troop transport and gunship

### TIE fighter

The Twin Ion Engine fighter is the Empire's standard starfighter, known for its distinctive scream and hexagonal wing panels.

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Manufacturer** | Sienar Fleet Systems |
| **Length** | 7.2 meters |
| **Armament** | 2 laser cannons |
| **Features** | No shields, no hyperdrive |

### TIE variants

- **TIE Interceptor** - Faster, more maneuverable version
- **TIE Bomber** - Heavy ordnance delivery
- **TIE Advanced x1** - Darth Vader's personal fighter
- **TIE Defender** - Elite fighter with shields and hyperdrive

### Imperial Star Destroyer

The iconic wedge-shaped capital ship that symbolizes Imperial might.

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Class** | Imperial-class Star Destroyer |
| **Length** | 1,600 meters |
| **Crew** | 37,000+ |
| **Armament** | Turbolasers, ion cannons, tractor beams |

## Legendary vessels

### Death Star

While technically a battle station, the Death Star deserves mention as the Empire's ultimate weapon.

> "The power to destroy a planet is insignificant next to the power of the Force." — Darth Vader

### Slave I (Firespray)

Boba Fett's distinctive patrol craft, later known as the Firespray, is one of the most recognizable bounty hunter ships in the galaxy.

### The Ghost

The VCX-100 light freighter serves as the mobile base for the Spectres crew in *Star Wars Rebels*.

## Jedi and Sith vessels

- **Jedi Starfighter** - Delta-7 and Eta-2 interceptors used during the Clone Wars
- **Sith Infiltrator** - Darth Maul's stealth ship
- **Kylo Ren's TIE Silencer** - Advanced First Order prototype

## The Razor Crest

Din Djarin's gunship from *The Mandalorian*, a pre-Imperial military patrol craft that becomes his home and transport for Grogu.

<Info>
The Razor Crest was designed to evoke the utilitarian aesthetic of the original trilogy while being distinct from other Star Wars vessels.
</Info>
