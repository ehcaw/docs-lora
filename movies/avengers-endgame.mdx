# Avengers: Endgame

**Avengers: Endgame** (2019) is a superhero film produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures. Directed by Anthony and Joe Russo, it serves as the epic conclusion to the Infinity Saga and the culmination of 22 interconnected Marvel Cinematic Universe films.

| **Box office** | $2.799 billion |

## Plot summary

Following the devastating events of *Avengers: Infinity War*, half of all life in the universe has been decimated by Thanos using the Infinity Stones. The remaining Avengers must find a way to reverse the destruction and restore balance to the universe.

The surviving heroes devise a daring plan involving time travel to collect the Infinity Stones from the past:

- **Tony Stark** cracks time travel using quantum mechanics
- **Scott Lang** provides the key insight about the Quantum Realm
- **The Avengers** split into teams to retrieve stones from different points in history
- **Nebula** inadvertently alerts past-Thanos to their plan

The film culminates in an epic final battle where heroes from across the MCU unite against Thanos and his army.

## Production

### Development

Endgame was filmed back-to-back with *Infinity War*, with principal photography beginning in August 2017. The Russo Brothers maintained extreme secrecy around the plot, providing actors with only their scenes and fake scripts.

### Filming

Production took place primarily at Pinewood Atlanta Studios in Fayette County, Georgia. Additional filming locations included Durham Cathedral in England (standing in for Asgard) and various locations in Atlanta.

## Reception

### Critical response

The film received critical acclaim for its direction, screenplay, emotional depth, visual effects, action sequences, and performances. Critics praised the film's ability to provide a satisfying conclusion to over a decade of storytelling.

### Box office success

Avengers: Endgame shattered numerous box office records:

- Highest-grossing film of all time (at $2.799 billion)
- Highest opening weekend ever ($357 million domestic)
- Fastest film to reach $1 billion (5 days)
- Fastest film to reach $2 billion (11 days)

## Legacy

Endgame marked the end of an era for the MCU:

- Concluded the Infinity Saga spanning 22 films over 11 years
- Provided closure for original Avengers characters
- Set the stage for new heroes and stories in Phase Four and beyond
- Demonstrated the unprecedented achievement of long-form cinematic storytelling
