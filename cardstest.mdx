# These are the new Typed Cards

## Basic Cards 
<Card type="info" title="Info">
  Neutral informational card — use when you just want to set something aside.
</Card>

<Card type="note" title="Note">
  Blue note card — additional context a reader may want.
</Card>

<Card type="tip" title="Tip">
  Green tip card — a best practice or helpful suggestion.
</Card>

<Card type="check" title="Check">
  Green check card — indicates a successful or completed state.
</Card>

<Card type="warning" title="Warning">
  Yellow warning card — something the reader should be careful about.
</Card>

<Card type="danger" title="Danger">
  Red danger card — destructive or irreversible action ahead.
</Card>
